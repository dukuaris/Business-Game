Interactive Solution Map Instructions


Begin by clicking on the box labeled Identify Gap in the top part of the screen. As you click on each box at the top, explanatory text will appear in the box at the bottom of the screen. Your path through this interactive solutions map will be identified by a red line and an arrow.

When you reach the end of a path, you will see several performance-enhancement tools from which to choose. Click on a tool for a short explanation of when the tool may be appropriate and then click on the button labeled "Implementation Choices" for more information on how to implement that tool.

You may click on the Identify Gap button to go back to the beginning at any time, you may click on any other box to "back up" a few levels or see other choices at the same level.
